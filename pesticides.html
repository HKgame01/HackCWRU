Classification of pesticides :

  • Pollution from cars, trucks, and other vehicles
  • Fossil fuel emissions from power plants
  • Clear cutting of forests
  • Water pollution is a major issue


Insecticides

  • Kill only insects they are sprayed or dusted.
  • Kill only insects that eat plants parts.
  • Transported within the plant and kill insects when they suck sap.
  • Kill insects that inhale toxic vapours.


Fungicides

  • Kill only fungi sprayed or dusted with the fungicide, or fungal spores, which come into contact with the fungicide.
  • transported within the plant and kills fungi growing within tissue of the plant.


Herbicides

  • Applied to the soil during the period after planting.
  • A herbicide that kills small weeds and seeds but leave the crop unharmed.
  • Applied to growing weeds after crop emergence.


Formulation

  • Pesticides prepared as large dry particles.
  • Pesticide mixed with edible products to form dry pellets, which are attractive to pests.
  • The pesticide is mixed with an oxidant and combustible material, which generates hot gas.
